PINE CITY MALL

Visit Pine City Mall for a wonderful shopping experience for the whole family featuring the best fashion, retail, leisure and entertainment all under one roof.

There are a variety of different stores at Pine City Mall, including well-known restaurants, supermarkets, clothing stores, shoe stores, electronic stores and jewellery stores.

Pine City Mall Food Court offers diverse dining options to suit every taste. With a range of eateries, including Halal and vegetarian choices, there's something to satisfy everyone.

Also don't forget to step into the world of cinema and catch the latest blockbusters and classics at the Pine City Mall Movie Theater.

Come down to Pine City Mall for a memorable shopping experience.
