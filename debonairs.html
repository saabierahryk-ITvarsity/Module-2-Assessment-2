Debonairs Pizza

About

Debonairs Pizza was established in 1991 by two university students in Pietermaritzburg. They created the Triple-Decker® and the Crammed-Crust® pizzas that so many South Africans know and love. Debonairs offers a variety of pizza flavours, toppings and crusts to choose from. Make your way to Debonairs Pine City Mall, where you can satisfy your pizza craving with a takeaway real-deal, standard or large pizza.
