Nando's

About

Nando's is a South African born restaurant chain known for its signature spicy Peri-Peri chicken. Nando's serves a delicious selection of grilled chicken options such wraps, burgers, salads, and more all made with their famous Peri-Peri basting. Come visit Nando's Pine City Mall if you are craving some delicious spicy flamed grilled chicken.
