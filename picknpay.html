Pick N Pay

About

Pick n Pay is one of South Africa's oldest and most beloved grocery stores. Pick n Pay provides fresh produce, essential groceries and other goods that are reasonably priced and offer excellent value for money. PnP Pine City Mall offers customers regular specials and discounts. Pick n Pay is the ideal choice for you, if you are looking for fresh ingredients for a spectacular supper, a tasty lunch or even a delicious snack. As a well-known brand, they strive to consistently uphold high standards of quality.
